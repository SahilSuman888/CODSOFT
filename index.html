Popular Workouts

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a great compound exercise that works your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core. They can be modified for different fitness levels.

Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are a challenging but rewarding exercise that targets your back, biceps, and forearms. They can be performed with an assisted pull-up machine for beginners.

Chin-Ups

Stand facing a bar. Grasp the bar from underneath with your arms in a tight, slightly closer than shoulder-width grip. Use your biceps to pull you up, bringing your head up over the bar.

Squats

Squats are a fundamental exercise that strengthens your quads, glutes, hamstrings, and core. They can be performed with variations like bodyweight squats, barbell squats, and goblet squats.

Lunges

Lunges are a great exercise for building lower body strength and improving balance. They can be performed with variations like walking lunges, stationary lunges, and reverse lunges.
